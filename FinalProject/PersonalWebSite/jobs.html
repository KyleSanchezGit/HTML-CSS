Dream Jobs

My dream jobs are: Freelance Coder, and Software Developer for Google and Amazon.

Freelance Coder

Being a master of all the different coding languages, companies contact me through my instagram or website to hire me for bits of code. Meanwhile I get to enjoy my home life, go to the gym on my own schedule, and make my own schedule.
Basically, being a Freelance Coder would give me the freedom to make my own schedule and make money on my own terms. Financial freedom and Time Freedom, this is something all of us want from our job. Of course, this type of job is unrealistic for a beginner in this undustry, but it is number one, in my dream jobs list.

Software Developer for Google

I picked this job, because it's Google. How many people can say, in their lifetime that they worked for the number one search engine on the planet.
I'm sure they pay well, have an enjoyable work environment, and there is room for promotion.

Software Developer for Amazon

It would be a real honor to help, or work on software programs for the company that I frequently buy products from. I use Amazon weekly to make orders, and learning how it works from the inside would really be a cool experience. I'm sure the pay and benefits are great and the work environment is probably enjoyable as well.
